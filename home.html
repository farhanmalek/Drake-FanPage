"So Far Gone" (2009) - This mixtape propelled Drake to stardom, featuring hits like "Best I Ever Had" and "Successful."

"Thank Me Later" (2010) - Drake's debut studio album featuring hits like "Over" and "Find Your Love."

"Take Care" (2011) - This critically acclaimed album includes singles like "Headlines" and "Take Care" ft. Rihanna.

"Nothing Was the Same" (2013) - Hits include "Started from the Bottom" and "Hold On, We're Going Home."

"If You're Reading This It's Too Late" (2015) - A surprise mixtape with tracks like "Energy" and "Know Yourself."

"What a Time to Be Alive" (2015) - A collaborative mixtape with Future, spawning hits like "Jumpman" and "Big Rings."

"Views" (2016) - Featuring "Hotline Bling" and "One Dance," it became a commercial success.

"More Life" (2017) - A playlist with hits like "Passionfruit" and "Fake Love."

"Scorpion" (2018) - This double album features "God's Plan" and "In My Feelings."

"Dark Lane Demo Tapes" (2020) - A mixtape including tracks like "Tootsie Slide" and "Pain 1993."

"Certified Lover Boy" (2021) - The latest release with hits like "Way 2 Sexy" and "Laugh Now Cry Later."
